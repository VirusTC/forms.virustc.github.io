# FDA Regulatory Disclaimer: General Wellness Module

## 1. Professional Judgment and Intended Use
This module functions strictly as a general wellness tool, administrative aid, or Non-Device application under FDA guidelines. It does not perform diagnostic pattern analysis, real-time physiological tracking, or predictive clinical interventions.

> **NOTICE:** This tool is intended solely to analyze, display, and organize general health information. It does not independently diagnose, cure, mitigate, prevent, or treat any specific disease, illness, or medical condition.

## 3. Formulaic Basis & Operational Limitations
Any health tracking, wellness scoring, or data aggregations provided by this system are calculated strictly utilizing models from existing medical formulas and equations. 

These formulaic models are intended for educational and general wellness tracking purposes only. They do not interpret complex clinical telemetry or provide active diagnostic guidance.

## 5. Data Privacy & Security (HIPAA)
All user and health data processed through this general wellness system is managed in accordance with applicable data privacy standards, HIPAA compliance requirements, and institutional data security policies. Users are responsible for maintaining secure access credentials to prevent unauthorized disclosure of personal health metrics.
